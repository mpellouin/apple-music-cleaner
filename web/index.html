Paste the two headers from your logged-in music.apple.com session. They stay between this server and Apple.

How do I find these?
  1. Open music.apple.com and sign in.
  2. Open DevTools — F12 or ⌘⌥I — and pick the Network tab.
  3. Filter for amp-api, then click around your Library until requests appear.
  4. Select any request → Request Headers:
    authorization → copy everything after “Bearer ”
    media-user-token → copy the whole value
